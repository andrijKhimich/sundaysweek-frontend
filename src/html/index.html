The Ultimate Whitsundays Sailing Tour

Rally your crew, select your dates, set sail.

The Whitsundays Week is an 8 day, 7 night yachting experience. Get a crew together, pick a yacht and join the flotilla for an exclusive week of sailing, partying and visiting picturesque locations with your closest friends and other like-minded travellers.

Your booking is complete with yacht, skipper, meals, and an unforgettable itinerary of daily activities, events and new destinations. This is a week you won’t forget.

Sail

There’s no better way to see the Whitsundays than by sailboat. Make the most of everything living on a yacht offers by eating, sleeping and living on a yacht for the week – no sailing experience needed. We take care of everything, with your skipper, meals and more included.

Discover

We’ve put together the ultimate itinerary for you to discover the best of the Whitsundays Island chain. Spend your days exploring secluded swim spots, hiking local rainforests, and of course, exploring the underwater world of the Great Barrier Reef.

Dance

The Whitsundays Week is whatever you want to make it. Party the night away on a stunning white-sand beach or lounge on a hammock under the stars. No matter how you choose to spend your week, we guarantee you won’t forget it.

How it works

Booking your Whitsundays Sailing Week is simple.

1. Rally your crew

Standard inclusions are covered in all booking options, including your skipper and meals.

Solo traveler

Book a solo spot and we’ll assign you to a cabin with another solo traveller in a same-sex bunk or cabin. It’s the perfect way to meet new people.

With a friend

Book a double cabin with a friend and you’ll be assigned to a yacht with other cabin bookings. Sail, discover and party with like minded travellers.

Book a Yacht

Travelling with a group? We offer a range of yachts sleeping up to 12 (including the skipper and optional host). The price of the yacht is fixed so simply split the cost between your friends

2. Choose your boat

FAQ

  • What is The Whitsundays Week

    The Whitsundays Week is a 8 day, 7 night, yachting experience in the Whitsundays. Sail the Whitsunday Islands with your friends and a group of other yachts with people from all over the world.

  • How do I join?
    • 1. Book a solo spot. This does not mean you will be travelling alone, we will assign you to a cabin with someone else who has also made a solo booking. It’s the perfect way to meet new people.
    • 2. Grab one other friend and book a double cabin. You will then be placed on a yacht with other people booking cabins. This is a great, simple package which includes the price of a skipper and food!
    • 3. Gather a group of friends and book a yacht. The price of the yacht is fixed, you split it between your crew members however you like.
  • I don't have any sailing experience is that alright

    No problem, we have experienced skippers to ensure you have the time of your life regardless of experience.
