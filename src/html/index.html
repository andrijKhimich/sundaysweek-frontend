FAQ

  • What is The Whitsundays Week

    The Whitsundays Week is a 8 day, 7 night, yachting experience in the Whitsundays. Sail the Whitsunday Islands with your friends and a group of other yachts with people from all over the world.

  • How do I join?
    • 1. Book a solo spot. This does not mean you will be travelling alone, we will assign you to a cabin with someone else who has also made a solo booking. It’s the perfect way to meet new people.
    • 2. Grab one other friend and book a double cabin. You will then be placed on a yacht with other people booking cabins. This is a great, simple package which includes the price of a skipper and food!
    • 3. Gather a group of friends and book a yacht. The price of the yacht is fixed, you split it between your crew members however you like.
  • I don't have any sailing experience is that alright

    No problem, we have experienced skippers to ensure you have the time of your life regardless of experience.
