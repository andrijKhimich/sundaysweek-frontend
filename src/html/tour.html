8 Day / 7 Night Whitsundays Tour

The ultimate Whitsundays tour, an exclusive week of sailing, partying and visiting picturesque locations with people from all around the world. Complete with skipper, yacht and an unforgettable itinerary. Get a crew together, pick a yacht and join the flotilla.

This tour is perfect for those travellers who want to see everything the Whitsundays has to offer and get to know your fellow travellers.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 Airlie Beach

    Your journey begins! You will join us at Airlie Beach where your skipper and yacht will be waiting for you. Here, you will meet your crew and have some time to explore Airlie Beach. We’ll have dinner together and get to know each other before heading off into our adventure, afterwards we can enjoy a few drinks and dance the night away!

  • Day 2 Hill Inlet

    Spectacular Whitehaven Beach, with its brilliant white silica sand which is among the purest in the world. This world-renowned inlet is the main stopping point for our tours, we will spend a full day here. Swim, snorkel, go stand up paddleboarding or hike, the walk to the lookout is an easy 15-minutes. From the lookout, you will see a panoramic view of the beach and island, truly a sight to be seen!

  • Day 3 Whitehaven Beach

    Explore the 7km long jewel of the Whitsundays, the spectacular Whitehaven Beach with its brilliant white silica sand which is among the purest in the world. Tourists travel from all corners of the globe to visit one of the world’s best beaches.

  • Day 4 Turtle Bay

    As its name suggests this bay on the southern end of Whitsunday Island is known for its frequent visits by green turtles. The nearby bays and beaches are a perfect place for a swim, stand up paddleboard or kayak. There are also spectacular views of Pentecost Island.

  • Day 5 Hamilton Island

    Hamilton Island, with its boutique shops, cafes and own unique vibe, is the perfect place to take a break or try something on the more adventurous side. The island resort offers countless activities and even boasts its own pool bar. Enjoy a gourmet dinner and dress up for one of The Whitsundays Week’s signature events, the Hamilton Island Soiree.

  • Day 6 Stonehaven

    One of the Whitsundays’ premier snorkelling destinations Stonehaven Bay and Langford Island will not disappoint! Slide off the back of your yacht into the warm turquoise waters, giving you the perfect opportunity to explore one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the world. Langford Island features a long sand spit which almost completely disappears at high tide, making it the perfect spot to chill.

  • Day 7 Blue Pearl Bay

    Hayman Island at its finest, well known by locals as excellent snorkelling and hiking location this is the perfect place for a last night send-off. Make sure you explore the many coral bombies as well as nearby caves and gorges.

  • Day 8 Airlie Beach return

    Today we return to Airlie Beach, the time has come to say goodbye to your skipper and crew, to pack your bags and leave your yacht. Thank you all for an amazing time and we look forward to welcoming you aboard again soon.
