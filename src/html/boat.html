Layout

Note: The exact layout of your yacht and cabin within the boat may differ from the image.

Luxury yachts can sleep up to 11 guests including the skipper. All cabins are twin share, the general layout for a yacht of this type is:

  • 3 double cabins
  • 1 bunk cabin
  • 1 saloon 2 spots

The saloon is the central living space it is a comunal area with a sofa bed. Some yacht may have a 4 cabin layout.

38 km 45 minute drive from Proserpine airport. Hamilton Island airport is also an option you transfers from the island to Airlie beach that run several times a day you can get more information on these here.
